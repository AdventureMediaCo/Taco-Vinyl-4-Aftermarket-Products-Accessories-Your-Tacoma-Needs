4 Aftermarket Products & Accessories Your Tacoma Needs

If you own a Toyota Tacoma, then you already know it’s a great truck. But what if we told you that it could be even better? With the aftermarket products and accessories available online at Taco Vinyl, you can equip your Tacoma with everything it needs to be a one-of-a-kind machine. Continue reading to discover four accessories that our customers describe as must-haves, then browse our store to find the perfect enhancements for your model!

Rock Sliders

Rock sliders are a set of bars that run along the sides of your vehicle. They are designed to protect your truck from damage while off-roading, deflecting rocks or tree branches that would otherwise hit your Tacoma’s underside. Not only is this added protection extremely valuable, but our rock sliders are also highly customizable, allowing you to achieve the rugged, aggressive look you’re going for.

Custom Grille Kits

First impressions matter, and your Tacoma’s grille is one of the first things that onlookers will notice. We would never complain about how these grilles look out of the factory, but we also think they’re a prime component to upgrade if you want to overhaul your truck’s appearance. With grille lights, grille badges, and custom grilles themselves, Taco Vinyl has everything needed to match your style.

Vinyl Decals

Vinyl decals are one of the best ways to add a bit of personality to your truck without breaking the bank. Our decals are made with high-quality vinyl that can endure the conditions you put your Tacoma through. They also come in an impressive assortment of styles and colors, so you can create a design that’s entirely unique.

Bed Racks

Finally, bed racks can be a great way to add extra storage to your truck. If you often find yourself carrying around a lot of gear, then a bed rack can help you keep everything organized. Our Tacoma bed racks fit model years 2005 to 2022, so you can be prepared for everything that lies on the road ahead.
